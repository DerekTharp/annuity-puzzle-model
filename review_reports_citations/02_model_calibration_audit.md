Short summary: The model/calibration block is broadly well sourced on the core economic mechanisms, but several numerical calibrations are either over-specific, mixed across related measures, or not directly supported by the cited text. The main issues are the Chetty risk-aversion range, the fixed-cost amount, the mixing of out-of-pocket and combined medical-spending moments, and a few places where the manuscript attributes a precise number to a paper whose local text only supports the broader idea.
